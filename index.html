About Waterparks

Waterparks is a really nice and super cool band that only the best people are capable of appreciating. The band formed in Houston, TX in 2011. Their music spands some niche genres, but can generally be categorized as alternative/pop punk. They will soon be on tour with My Chemical Romance.

Band Members

Awsten Knight -lead vocals, guitar, programming

Geoff Wigington -lead guitar, backing vocals

Otto Wood -drums, backing vocals
